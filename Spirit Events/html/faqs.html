Frequently Asked Questions

  • Spirit Events is a platform that connects event organizers with attendees. We provide a platform for event organizers to create and manage events, and for attendees to discover and register for events.

  • To register for an event, simply click on the "Register" button on the event page and follow the prompts. You will need to provide some basic information about yourself, such as your name and email address.

  • Our refund policy varies depending on the event. Please check the event page for specific refund policies.

  • You can contact the event organizer through the "Contact" button on the event page.

  • Our cancellation policy varies depending on the event. Please check the event page for specific cancellation policies.

  • To reset your password, simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on our login page and follow the prompts.

  • We accept all major cards, mobile money and others required. To know more you can contact the event Contact.

  • To get a refund, please contact the event organizer through the "Contact" button on the event page.

  • Ticketing policies vary depending on the event. Please check the event page for specific ticketing policies.
